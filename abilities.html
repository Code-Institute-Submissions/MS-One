Every Champion has 18 skill points available to spend on their abilities. 1 skill points become available after every level-up, and you level up by gaining Experience Points (Exp) in game. Exp is gained through killing minions/ jungle camps/ epic monsters and by slaying your opponents.

Lucian's optimum skill path is showcased here.

Precision Rune Path

Rune Choice

Runes highlighted with a gold border display Lucian's standard precision Rune path.

Press the Attack:

After landing 3 auto-attacks on a enemy champion, deal an additional 40-180 damage (based on level) whilst also increasing all damage that target receives for the next 6 seconds by 8% - 12%. Best rune for Lucian as he can quickly proc 3 auto-attacks and deal the additional damage.

Truimph:

Gain back a percentage of missing health after slaying an enemy. Helps to keep you alive especially during teamfights

Bloodline-tenacity:

As the game progresses, gain tenacity upon levels. This will help you escape CC faster and stay alive longer.

Cut Down:

Deal additional damage upon reaching 30% HP. Dealing more damage will increase your chances of survival whilst also increasing the amount you vamp.

Passive - lightslinger
After casting an ability, Lucian's next auto-attack will be a double shot: one from each of his pistols
Q - Piercing Light
Cast on a target within Lucian's auto attack-range, he fires a bolt of light which pierces his target and hits those a short distance behind it
W - Ardent Blaze
After a brief channel, Lucian fires a cross shaped projectile that expands at the end of its range. Enemies hit by this are marked with light and if Lucian attacks an enemy marked with light he will momentarily gain a small amount of movement speed (Great for kiting)
E - Relentless pursuit
Lucian dashes towards the cursor upon activation of Relentless Pursuit. The cooldown of this ability is lowered by 2 seconds for every shot of his double shot landed whilst this ability is on cooldown
R - The Culling
Lucian fully unleashes his weapons, firing continously in a straight line, damaging the first target hit in his extended range. His direction of attack is fixed whilst channeling this ability
