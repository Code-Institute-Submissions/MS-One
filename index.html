Champ Guides is a page focused on helping League of Legends players understand more about their chosen champs. With over 140 champions currently in the game, most pages only focus on the statistics of a champ. Here we'll try to increase your understanding of certain champs and the best way to play them

League of Legends is a 5 v 5 MOBA (Multiplayer Online Battle Arena). Each player occupies a role in their team and each role usaully starts the game in their lane: Either Top, Mid-lane, Jungle and Bot-lane. Bot-lane is a 2 v 2 lane, with one player on a champion that scales well into the mid/late(Traditionally named the ADC - Attack Damage Carry) game (by farming gold and objectives) and the other on a champ that facilitates this.

With one of the biggest eSports scenes in competitive gaming and boasting numbers of up to 8 million daily players, League of Legends is a vast game. However, the learning curve can be steep once you start playing against other players, especially when you factor in that every champ has their own unique kit and abilities. Use of this page assumes basic knowledge in League of Legends and how it's played but do not worry, concepts will always be explained with the intention of helping new players. Gifs and videos showcase the kind of skills and outplays you can achieve once you've become familiar with your champ and their limits.
